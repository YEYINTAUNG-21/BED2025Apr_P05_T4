    About Sudoku

    Sudoku is a classic number puzzle that helps stimulate brain activity and improve logical thinking. The goal is to fill a 9x9 grid with numbers so that each column, row, and 3x3 subgrid contains the numbers 1-9 without repetition.

    Sudoku is great for aging minds because it helps sharpen memory, enhances cognitive skills, and provides a sense of accomplishment when completing puzzles. Playing Sudoku regularly can boost brain health and increase mental agility, keeping your mind active and sharp.
